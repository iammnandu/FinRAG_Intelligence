Fraud Incident Response Playbook - Banking

1) Card Fraud Detection
- Trigger when transaction velocity exceeds historical baseline by 3x within 20 minutes.
- Trigger when the same card is attempted in geographically impossible locations in under 60 minutes.
- Immediate action: put card on temporary hold and request step-up verification.

2) Account Takeover (ATO)
- Risk indicators: password reset from unknown device, SIM swap alerts, unusual beneficiary additions.
- Immediate action: lock outbound transfers for 30 minutes, challenge with MFA, notify customer.
- Escalate to Tier-2 fraud analyst if more than two high-risk indicators are present.

3) Phishing Campaigns
- If customer reports phishing SMS or email, capture sender details and URL.
- Block known domains at secure web gateway and submit indicators to SOC intel platform.
- Mandatory customer advisory: never share OTP, CVV, PIN, or full card number over calls or links.

4) Regulatory Logging
- Keep fraud event logs for a minimum of 7 years.
- Log investigator actions, user communications, and decision rationale for each incident.
- Preserve immutable audit trails for internal and external compliance reviews.
